Research Interests

My research is developed at the intersection of Education, Applied Mathematics and Management, with a particular focus on the use of mathematical modelling, optimization techniques, statistics and quantitative methods to support decision-making processes in complex organizational contexts.

My current research interests include the application of operations research and optimization models to human resources planning and management in the education sector, aiming at the efficient allocation of scarce resources and the improvement of organizational performance.
